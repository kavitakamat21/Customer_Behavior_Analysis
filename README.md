## About the Dataset  :-
- Type: Retail / E-commerce Dataset
- Nature: Structured (Tabular)
- Level: Customer Transaction Level
- Data Mix:
  Numerical → Age, Purchase Amount, Previous Purchases
  Categorical → Category, Payment Method, Season
  
✨This dataset is used for customer behavior analysis + business decision-making

## It includes fields like:
- Customer ID  – Unique customer_id 
- Age  – Each Customer age 
- Gender - Male, Femal, other
- Item Purchased - Sub-Category of product 
- Purchase Amount (USD) - prodcut cost in USD
- Location  – Customer Locations
- Season - winter, Autumn, Spring etc
- Review Rating - Ratings of products
- Subscription Status - subscriptions yes/no
- Discount Applied - Discounts on Products
- Payment Method - UPI,Debit Card Cash etc

## Domain Background:-
In the e-commerce industry, companies track every customer interaction:
- What customers buy
- How often do they buy
- How much do they spend
- Whether they respond to discounts

## Findings:-
- customer purchasing behavior is strongly influenced by demographic factors, product preferences, and spending patterns
- A small group of high-value customers contributes a significant share of total revenue
- Indicating the importance of customer retention and personalized marketing strategies.
- Revenue analysis shows that certain customer segments, age groups, and product categories consistently generate higher sales,
- while low-performing segments contribute less to business growth.
- 📎This suggests that the company should focus more on profitable customer groups and optimize marketing campaigns for underperforming segments.

## Business Recommendations :-
1.Focus retention strategies on high-value and repeat customers.
2.Increase personalized marketing using customer segmentation.
3.Promote best-selling products more aggressively

## Final Outcome :-
